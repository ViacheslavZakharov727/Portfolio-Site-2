About Me

Hi, I’m Viacheslav Zakharov — a 16‑year‑old designer from Kyiv, now based in Uster. Design came into my life early: I loved how color and simple shapes could change the feeling of a page. Today I bring that curiosity into every project. I like clean layouts, clear hierarchy, and small details that make people smile. I work with Jimdo, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Canva — and, when a project needs structure, Word, PowerPoint and Excel. Most of the work in this portfolio is self‑initiated. I use it to practice ideas, train my eye, and discover what good design means to me. My goal is simple: create warm, modern, honest visuals — the kind that communicate without shouting and stay memorable after you close the tab.
